Our series of talks from distinguished invited speakers provide everyone interested in helping the world with limited resources with valuable information. We live in an age when most of us can save many human lives at minimal costs. Come to EAGxPrague to learn how to use scientific thinking and clear reasoning to fulfill our altruistic goals! Let's build a better world together!

Topic: Effective altruism

Reducing suffering with reason and evidence.

Topic: Technological progress and the future of humanity

Will our technologies lead to unprecedented progress? Can we deal with unexpected risks from fast development?

Topic: Development aid

Evidence-based solutions that provably helping hundreds of millions in developing countries.

Topic: Decision-making and rationality

To reach correct conclusions and take the best possible actions, it is crucial to use clear and correct reasoning. Which systematic harmful biases do people have in general, and how can we prevent them from leading us astray?

Topic: Choosing a beneficial career

We spend 80 000 hours of our lives at work. How can we use this short span of time to help others?
